徐浩 | Hao Xu

I am a MPhil student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), studying physics-based simulation in computer graphics.

Computer Graphics is an interesing interdisplinary field. I feel so fascinated when I found that by using math representations, simulating physical laws, implementing by engineering expertise, and adding a little artistic control, we're able to re-create the world in computers. I'm most interested about mimicking nature's physical laws side, which technically leads me towards methods that connect physical modeling, numerical simulation, controllable dynamics, efficient implementation, and high-quality visual output.

Before moving into graphics simulation, I worked on computer vision at Sun Yat-sen University's HCP Lab, and on LLM-powered procedural 3D generation at Shanghai AI Lab, mentored by Dr. Zhaoyang Lyu.
